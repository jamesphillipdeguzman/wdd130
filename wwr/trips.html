White Water Rafting

Our Adventure Trips Package

BEGINNER

Duration 1 day

Highlights: Scenic one-day rafting with mild rapids, wildlife viewing, and picnics. Suitable for beginners. Guided trips available for a hassle-free experience.

Additional Activities: Swimming, exploring hiking trails, and enjoying riverside picnics.

INTERMEDIATE

Duration 1-2 days

Highlights: Varied one to two-day rafting with calm stretches and exciting rapids. Wildlife encounters and overnight camping options. Suitable for all levels, guided trips ensure safety and enjoyment.

Additional Activities: Fishing for salmon or steelhead, relaxing at riverside campsites, and birdwatching along the riverbanks.

ADVANCED

Duration 1-2 days

Highlights: Picturesque one to two-day rafting through forests and farmland. Wildlife spotting, swimming, and family-friendly fun. Guided trips offered for a memorable experience suitable for all ages.

Additional Activities: Visiting scenic viewpoints, swimming in shallow sections, and exploring nearby waterfalls or hot springs.
